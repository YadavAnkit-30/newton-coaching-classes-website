About Newton Coaching Classes

Newton Coaching Classes provides quality education for students from 8th to 12th standard, covering both CBSE and SSC boards. For college students, we specialize exclusively in the Science stream. Our goal is to empower students with strong subject knowledge, personal guidance, and expert mentorship.

We believe in concept-based learning and providing individual attention to ensure every student excels academically.
